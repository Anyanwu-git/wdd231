NP Membership Benefits

  • No annual fee for qualifying non-profit organizations.
  • Listing in the online member directory.
  • Access to community events and networking opportunities.
  • Discounted access to selected training sessions.

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • Affordable annual membership fee.
  • Business listing with logo in the directory.
  • Invitations to monthly networking events.
  • Member newsletter and community updates.

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze benefits.
  • Spotlight feature on the home page on a rotating basis.
  • Discounts on chamber-sponsored workshops.
  • Advertising opportunities in chamber emails.

Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver benefits.
  • Premium placement in the directory and spotlight section.
  • Priority sponsorship opportunities for major events.
  • Exclusive invitations to leadership roundtables.
